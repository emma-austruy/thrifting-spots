Welcome to Thrifting In France, your go-to resource for thrifting in France!

Our mission is to provide you with everything you need to know about thrifting, vintage fashion, and sustainable shopping practices in France.
Founded by a group of dedicated thrifters and vintage fashion enthusiasts, Thrifting In France is here to help you discover hidden gems, unique finds, and incredible deals in thrift stores across France.

What you'll find on our website:

  • Comprehensive guides to the best thrift stores in France

  • Curated recommendations for vintage fashion lovers

  • Insider tips and tricks for successful thrift shopping

  • Exclusive discounts and deals from our partners

We believe that thrifting is not only about scoring amazing bargains but also about embracing individual style, reducing environmental impact, and supporting a more sustainable fashion industry.

Join our thrifting community and share your thrifting adventures, tips, and favorite finds with us! Connect with us on social media for daily inspiration and updates.
For any questions, collaborations, or feedback, feel free to reach out to us at thriftinginfrance@jmail.com.

Happy thrifting!
